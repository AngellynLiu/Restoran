Our Story

The Story Behind "Joycious"

Joycious was founded by childhood friends Novi, James Wilkinson, Angellyn, and Mayseila, who shared a vision of creating a place where great food and joyful memories come together. Inspired by their hometown’s warmth and community spirit, they designed a cozy yet modern restaurant that offers a blend of locally sourced ingredients and international flavors. The name "Joycius" reflects their mission to bring happiness through every dish. With a commitment to sustainability and exceptional service, Joycius quickly became a beloved spot for both locals and visitors, offering a welcoming atmosphere where every meal feels like a celebration.
